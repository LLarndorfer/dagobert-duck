Entenhausen: Donald Duck's Hometown

Entenhausen is where all the fun happens for Donald Duck and his friends. It's their hometown, filled with adventures and mishaps. In Entenhausen, you'll find Donald's cozy spot, and he hangs out with his family and friends here. There's always something going on, whether it's a quirky event or a mischievous plan by Donald's nephews, Tick, Trick, and Track. The town has its own charm, and you can explore places like Duckburg where Donald lives. There's also the famous Money Bin of Donald's rich uncle, Dagobert Duck, filled with more money than you can imagine. So, Entenhausen is not just a place; it's the backdrop for all the laughter, chaos, and fun in the world of Donald Duck!
